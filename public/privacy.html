BuiltByHuman

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

BuiltByHuman ("we", "our", or "us") operates the BuiltByHuman Chrome extension and the website at builtbyhuman.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Website content or page source code from your browser
  • Authentication credentials
  • Financial or payment information

2. How the Extension Works

When you click the BuiltByHuman extension button, the URL of the active tab is sent to the BuiltByHuman API (builtbyhuman.app) to perform an AI authorship analysis. Only the URL you explicitly choose to scan is transmitted. No data is sent passively or in the background.

3. API Requests

Scan requests are sent to our API hosted at builtbyhuman.app. We may log URLs submitted for scanning for the purposes of improving our detection model. These logs are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services within the extension.

5. Children's Privacy

BuiltByHuman is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Summary

BuiltByHuman only transmits the URL you choose to scan. We collect no personal data, track no browsing history, and sell nothing to anyone.
